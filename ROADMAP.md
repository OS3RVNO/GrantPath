# Roadmap

## Phase 0: MVP Stabilization

- keep the MVP inventory honest
- reduce regressions in primary flows
- improve setup, notices and operator guidance
- keep quality gates green

## Phase 1: Sellable MVP

- close the main vertical slice:
  - filesystem collection
  - explainability
  - who-has-access
  - what-if
  - report export
  - review campaign
- make first-run onboarding smoother
- strengthen operational visibility for scans and freshness

## Phase 2: Pilot Enterprise

- harden production deployment defaults
- improve runbooks and backup/restore
- keep worker split and job ownership stable
- improve search, graph and access query ergonomics

## Phase 3: Enterprise Foundation

- use `OpenSearch`, `Valkey`, `ClickHouse` more deeply in runtime
- formalize support matrix for connectors
- improve RBAC and operational governance
- extend observability and performance visibility

## Phase 4: Game-Changer Engine

- stronger incremental recomputation
- richer materialized access structures
- better path ranking and graph reasoning
- faster large-scope simulations

## Phase 5: Game-Changer Product

- richer risk scoring
- stronger evidence and historical drift analysis
- denser investigation tooling
- deeper governance flows
- broader enterprise connector coverage
